Introduction

The programs in this page are example Algol68G programs. To study Algol 68 in detail, next references are suggested. Some of them are available on-line.

  • [Revised Report 1976] A. van Wijngaarden et al., editors, Revised Report on the Algorithmic Language Algol 68. Acta Informatica 5 1-236 [1976]; SIGPLAN Notices 12(5) 1-70 [1977].
    The Revised Report is the formal defining document; as such it was not meant as a practical guide to Algol 68 programming and it has been said that few people learned Algol 68 programming by studying this report alone. Wilhelm Kloke has made available an on-line version of the Revised Report.
  • Sian Leitch wrote a good informal textbook on Algol 68: Programming Algol 68 Made Easy.
    Roy Fullmer has posted an on-line version of this textbook.
  • [Brailsford 1979] D.F. Brailsford, A.N. Walker. Introductory Algol 68 programming. Ellis Horwood Ltd, Chichester [1979].
  • [Lindsey 1971] C.H. Lindsey, S.G. van der Meulen. Informal introduction to ALGOL 68. North Holland Publishing Company [1971].
  • [Koster 1978, 1981] C.H.A. Koster, H. Meijer. Systematisch programmeren in Algol 68. Deel 1, 2. (Dutch). Kluwer, Deventer [1978][1981].
  • [McGettrick 1978] A. D. McGettrick. Algol 68: a first and second course. Cambridge University Press [1978].
  • Norman Hardy has a page explaining ideas behind Algol 68, and an elaborated example of Algol 68 applied in tensor calculations.
